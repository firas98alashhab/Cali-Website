    Mixed Reality

    Mixed Reality (MR) is an advanced visualization technology that combines elements of both virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) to create immersive and interactive digital experiences. It enables users to interact with and manipulate digital content within their real-world environment, blurring the lines between the physical and virtual worlds.
